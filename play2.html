CLI

I should be most unfortunate without your assistance, Sir, for your wife has rejected my offer, and, her mind being prepossessed in favour of Trissotin, she insists upon having him for a son-in-law.

CHRY.

But what fancy is this that she has got into her head? Why in the world will she have this Mr. Trissotin?

ARI.

It is because he has the honour of rhyming with Latin that he is carrying it off over the head of his rival.
